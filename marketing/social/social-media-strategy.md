# Social Media Strategy

Comprehensive social media strategy and content calendar for Masterful, including platform-specific approaches, visual guidelines, and ready-to-use content templates.

## Brand Voice on Social

**Tagline:** "Be Kind to Your Mind"

**Tone:** Warm, calming, empowering. Never preachy. Speaks like a thoughtful friend who genuinely cares about mental wellness.

**Voice attributes:**
- Gentle authority (evidence-informed, not clinical)
- Inclusive and accessible (meditation is for everyone)
- Aspirational calm (the feed itself should feel like a micro-meditation)

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Description | Mix |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| **Mindful Moments** | Daily quotes, micro-meditations, breathing prompts, calming visuals | 35% |
| **App & Feature Spotlights** | Screenshots, feature walkthroughs, update announcements, tips | 20% |
| **Wellness Science** | Research-backed insights on meditation, sleep, stress, neuroplasticity | 20% |
| **Community & Stories** | User testimonials, "why I meditate" prompts, community engagement | 15% |
| **Behind the Scenes** | Dev journey, founder story, app design process, team values | 10% |

## Platform Strategies

### X/Twitter (@masterful\_app)

**Current state:** Automated quote posting every 6 hours from `dailyQuotes` collection.

**Enhanced strategy:**
- Keep the automated daily quotes (consistent presence)
- Add original content between automated posts
- Engage in wellness/meditation conversations, reply to relevant threads
- Use threads for wellness science deep-dives (1–2x/week)

**Posting cadence:** 2–3 posts/day (1 automated quote + 1–2 original)

**Content types:**
- Automated daily quotes (existing infrastructure)
- Quick tips / breathing exercises
- Feature spotlights with App Store link
- Wellness science snippets with source citations
- Engagement prompts
- Weekly threads (e.g., "5 signs you need a mental reset")

**Hashtag strategy (rotate, don't overuse):**
- Primary: #Masterful #BeKindToYourMind
- Secondary: #Meditation #Mindfulness #MentalWellness #MeditationApp #Wellness

### Instagram (@masterful)

**Visual identity:**
- Dark backgrounds (#0a0a1a navy base) with cyan (#00ffff) and purple (#8a2be2) gradients
- Glassmorphism-style card overlays for text content
- Clean, minimal typography (Playfair Display for headlines, sans-serif for body)
- Consistent color palette matching the app's UI

**Posting cadence:** 5–7 feed posts/week + daily Stories

**Feed posts:**
- Quote cards (dark bg, glassmorphism overlay, cyan/purple accents)
- Carousel posts: wellness science breakdowns
- App screenshots with glassmorphism frame mockups
- Reels: 15–30s guided breathing exercises, ambient nature + meditation prompts
- Feature announcement graphics

**Stories (daily):**
- Morning check-in polls
- Quick tips / "Did you know?" facts
- App feature demos (screen recordings)
- User testimonial reshares
- "This or that" engagement stickers

**Highlights:**
- "Get Started" — onboarding tips
- "Features" — app walkthroughs
- "Science" — wellness research highlights
- "Community" — user stories
- "Tips" — quick meditation techniques

## Visual Style Guide

### Post Templates

1. **Quote card:** Dark bg, centered quote text in white, author attribution in cyan, subtle glassmorphism border glow
2. **Tip card:** Dark bg, numbered list with cyan bullet accents, purple gradient header bar
3. **Feature spotlight:** App screenshot on dark bg, feature name in bold white, description in white/70
4. **Science fact:** Dark bg, statistic in large cyan text, supporting text in white/70, source citation
5. **Engagement prompt:** Dark bg with gradient orb visual, question in bold white

## Recommendations

1. **Populate `dailyQuotes` consistently** — the X automation depends on fresh quotes
2. **Create visual templates in Canva/Figma** matching the style guide
3. **Set up Instagram Business account** with proper bio and App Store link
4. **Leverage the influencer/referral system** already built — target micro-influencers (1K–50K followers)
5. **Consider Instagram auto-posting** via existing Cloud Functions infrastructure
6. **Track UTM parameters** on all social links to measure conversion
7. **Engage daily** — reply to comments, like relevant posts, join conversations
